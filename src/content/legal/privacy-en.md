# TitiLinks — Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 22, 2026**

This policy explains what information TitiLinks ("we," "us") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to titilinks.com and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Account information.** When you sign up: email address, password (stored as a secure hash by our authentication provider — we never see your plaintext password), display name, username/handle, and plan tier.

**Content you create.** Your page content: bio, links, text, images, video, uploaded fonts, themes, and settings (including badge and content-gating preferences). Your public page is, by design, public.

**Payment information.** Paid subscriptions are processed by Stripe. Your card details go directly to Stripe and never touch our servers. We receive limited billing metadata from Stripe (such as subscription status, plan, and renewal dates) to operate your account. Stripe's handling of your data is governed by Stripe's privacy policy.

**Analytics events.** We record page views and link clicks on creator pages (including which link was clicked and when, and general technical data such as browser type, device type, and referrer) to power creators' analytics dashboards and to operate and improve the Service.

**Visitor emails (creator pages).** If a creator uses the email-subscribe block and you submit your email on their page, we store that email on the creator's behalf and make it available to that creator. The creator is responsible for how they use it. If you want your email removed from a creator's list, contact the creator, or contact us and we will assist.

**Referral data.** If you sign up through a referral link, we record which account referred you, to operate the referral program.

**Technical data.** Standard server logs (IP address, timestamps, requested pages) for security and operations.

## 2. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not sell your personal information. We do not collect card numbers. We do not use your content to train AI models.

## 3. How We Use Information

To provide and operate the Service (render pages, process subscriptions, show analytics); to communicate with you about your account, security, and material changes; to prevent fraud and abuse (including referral abuse); to improve the Service; and to comply with law.

## 4. Third-Party Processors

We use a small set of service providers to run TitiLinks:

- **Supabase** — authentication, database, and file storage
- **Stripe** — payment processing
- **Vercel** — website hosting and delivery

Each processes data only as needed to provide their service to us. Creator pages may also contain third-party content the creator chose to embed (for example, links or tracking pixels a creator configures); those third parties' collection is governed by their own policies, and the creator's page — not TitiLinks — determines their presence.

## 5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies/local storage for essential functions: keeping you signed in, remembering language preference, and remembering choices such as an adult-content interstitial confirmation. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

## 6. Data Retention

Account and content data are retained while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal, security, or billing-record purposes. Analytics events are retained to provide historical dashboards while the associated account exists.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise most of these directly in the app (edit profile, delete content, delete account) or by contacting us at support@titilinks.com. We respond to verified requests within the timeframe required by applicable law. We do not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

## 8. International Users

TitiLinks is operated from the United States and data is processed in the United States (and wherever our processors operate). If you use the Service from outside the US — including from Colombia and elsewhere in Latin America — you understand your information is transferred to and processed in the US under this policy.

## 9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. Adult-content features require users to be 18+. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

## 10. Security

We use industry-standard measures: encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, access controls, and row-level security on our database. No system is perfectly secure; protect your credentials and notify us of any suspected breach of your account.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. For material changes we will provide notice (email or in-app). The effective date above always reflects the current version.
